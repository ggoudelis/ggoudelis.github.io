about me.


Im currently working with Deluxesign3D using Robotic 3D printing via Fused Granular Fabrication (FGF) to manufacture large aesthetical and functional projects.

My passion lies in creating things that I believe should exist in this world and design for me is the best medium to transform this from a desire into a reality. Without a doubt, design has the capability to transform the world both physically and emotionally. However, I believe that to be able to leave a positive impact, it is necessary for one to have control of the technology behind design. Only in this way can the boundaries of creativity be truly pushed and broken.

With a masters in both Mechanical Engineering from UCL and Industrial Design from Politecnico di Milano, my approach in design has developed into combining a balance of technical and creative methods. Of course, the challenge in todayâ€™s multidimensional world is having the right balance, and this should be different for every project, problem or setting. My experience in both scientific and creative environments offers me a unique perspective on this, having worked in a high-tech IoT industry, design studio and digital fabrication lab. Moreover, as a firm believer in pushing boundaries, I have also invested a considerable amount of time in the research field and have also successfully published scientific papers. Always being curious and having lived in 5 different countries, I have experienced a multitude of cultures, from which I have learnt, drawn inspiration and to this day base my emotional language on.
