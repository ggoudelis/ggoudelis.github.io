about me.


I am currently finishing my masters in Design & Engineering at Politecnico di Milano.

My passion lies in creating things with meaning, and product design is the best way for me to achieve this. I have been through a technical education, but since I was young I have always thought in a creative and abstract way. As a result, my approach in design is the combination of problem solving and creaticity, with the goal of delivering an inclusive and purposeful output. Additionally, having lived in many countries, I have experienced a multitude of cultures, from which I try to learn and draw inspiration. This also allows me to work efficiently in multidisciplinary teams, as I am able to quickly adapt and fit into different environments.

My academic background is in Mechanical Engineering (UCL) and Industrial Design (Politecnico di Milano), both completed at a master's level. I have a total of 18 months of industry experience in a high-tech company (EnOcean GmbH), a design studio (CorsiRhino) and a digital fabricaiton lab (Direct3D). Additionally, I have also spent 9 months in the technical research field, publishing a scientific paper on the performance of solar panels. In my free time I enjoy working on projects that allow me to explore my creative boundaries through various types of creations.
