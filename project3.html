THE SOLUTION



















The circuit is split in the components that work with 220 V directly from the plug and those that operate at 5V through a transformer. There are 3 heating elements and each of them consume 500 W. HE1 and HE2 work when the device is turned on, while HE3 activates when the slider is pushed down using a transistors set up.

The 20 minute timer is kept by a capacitor whose charging rate is set by 3 resistors. When the capacitor reaches a certain charge, the transistors turn off HE3 that causes the electromagnet to release the slider.
