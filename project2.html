MSc Group Project

(01)
ABOUT THE PROJECT

(02)
THE PROBLEM

    Even though coffee cups and plastic bottles are both fully recyclable, they are hopelessly sent to landfill in most cases. It has been estimated by WRAP (2016) that in the UK, 16 million plastic bottles and 7 million disposable cups are sent to landfill every day. One of the big challenges of recycling, is that waste sent to facilities, needs to be as pure as possible and free of contaminants to ensure it will be recycled.

(03)
THE SOLUTION

    The product designed in this project can differentiate and sort both from other waste, using image recognition with machine learning. The business plan in this case is to collaborate with big coffee companies like COSTA, who will fund its production and provide a reward scheme for the users who successfully recycle using the machine.
